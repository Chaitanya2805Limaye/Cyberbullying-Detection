In today's digital age, cyberbullying has become a widespread issue, impacting individuals from all walks of life. But what if we could revolutionize how we combat it? Enter our groundbreaking cyberbullying prediction model, driven by advanced social media analytics. By delving into demographic data like age, religion, gender, and ethnicity, alongside past cyberbullying instances and positive interactions, our algorithm offers unparalleled accuracy in forecasting potential bullying scenarios. But we don't stop there. We empower users to actively participate in the process, allowing them to provide feedback and lodge complaints post-prediction. This unique blend of cutting-edge technology and human insight not only identifies at-risk individuals but also fosters a community-driven approach to prevention.
